Staircases

The staircase is often the highest traffic area. A carpeted staircase could wear out in a few years, but a hardwood staircase could last a lifetime. A staircase is usually the first thing you see when you walk into a house. Its majestic look can leave an unforgettable impression, not to mention it would blend in perfectly with your new or existing hardwood floor.

Pacific Carpet and Flooring can help you convert a staircase into hardwood and match the wood to the floor around it at an affordable price.

Ask at our showroom for help with the many available options.
