Vinyl

Vinyl flooring comes in a fantastic selection of colours and patterns including stone, ceramic and wood look. It is easy to install, easy to maintain, water resistant, and durable.

Vinyl flooring can be used almost anywhere. It's a practical choice for areas that are exposed to moisture, or require frequent cleaning like kitchens, bathrooms, laundry areas, and work areas. It is both versatile and affordable so it continues to grow in popularity.
