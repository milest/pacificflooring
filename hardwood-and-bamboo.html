Hardwood Flooring

Nothing compares to the elegance of a gorgeous hardwood floor. Wood is so rich and warm it can work in any room of your house. With our enormous selection you're sure to find the ideal floor for your home right here.

We feature hardwood floors in an incredible range of finishes, colours and types. Some of our most popular floors are red or white oak, maple, ash and yellow birch. In addition, we have a wide selection of exotic woods.

Hardwood floors are excellent for high-traffic areas because of its resilience and ease of maintenance. With the right care, a hardwood floor can last for generations.

Bamboo

Bamboo is an eco-friendly choice. Since bamboo regenerates from the roots and does not need to be replanted like trees, it avoids soil erosion and deforestation. Ready for harvest in a few years, the plant poses less environmental impact.

Bamboo is comparable in hardness to most traditional hardwoods, making it an excellent choice for high traffic areas as well. Bamboo flooring is resistant to moisture, stains and mildew. It is easy to install, durable, and doesn't warp, cup or gap. It also meets commercial and residential fire prevention standards.

Most of Pacific Carpet and Flooring's hardwood and bamboo products come with a minimum of 25 years warranty.
