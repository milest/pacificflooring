Laminate Floors

Laminate flooring reproduces the look of natural hardwood flooring without the cost. Its smooth hard surface is remarkably easy to clean, resists fading, and is incredibly durable.

With its pleasing textured look, low maintenance, easy installation and easier pricing, laminate flooring can be an attractive alternative to hardwoods. It's no wonder laminate is one of the fastest growing flooring options available.

Cork

Cork is a natural material from the bark of cork oaks. Since it can be harvested every 7 years or so without killing the trees, it is an environmentally friendly choice. Cork floors are amazingly soft and easy to walk on. Plus they offer high insulation and sound absorption qualities that help keep your home warm and quiet. It is durable, very resilient, and can last for generations. Being hypoallergenic, it's also a fantastic choice for many people who suffer from allergies.
